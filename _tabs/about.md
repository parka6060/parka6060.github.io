Hey stranger, welcome to my digital writing nook. I'm En, the artist behind eninabox. This is where I jot down some of my thoughts.

This site is a little bit like my personal journal, you can read more about why this site exists [here]({% post_url 2023-12-28-a-place-to-write %}). Here you will find a mix of writing on whatever catches my interest. There is no overaching theme, just meandering paths of things I find interesting.Some posts are fully formed ideas, while others are brief notes or work in progress. I'm not a good writer, so any good ideas I might have are probably borrowed, and the bad ones are my own.

Stay wonderful and thanks for visiting my corner of the internet.
